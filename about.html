I am a graduate student at Tufts University, pursuing a joint PhD in Computer Science and Cognitive Science, working in the Human-Robot Interaction Lab (HRILab) with Professor Matthias Scheutz. I am originally from Ontario, Canada, and my undergraduate studies were at the University of Waterloo, where I double-majored in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science.

My current work at the HRILab deals principally with incorporating moral and social norms into artificial agents.

When I am not doing research, I may be singing in an a cappella group, or enjoying (and more recently acting in) local community theater.
